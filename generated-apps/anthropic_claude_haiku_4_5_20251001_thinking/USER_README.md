# Zeeter User Guide

## Welcome to Zeeter
Zeeter is a simple, modern social network where you can share your thoughts in 280 characters or less. Follow friends, engage with their posts, and stay updated with notifications.

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account
1. Click "Sign Up" on the home page
2. Enter your email and password (minimum 6 characters)
3. Add a display name (optional but recommended)
4. Complete profile setup with bio and avatar URL (both optional)
5. Start posting!

## Main Features

### Home Feed
- **View Posts**: Scroll through posts from all users
- **Create Post**: Write up to 280 characters in the text box at the top
- **Search**: Use the search box to find posts by keyword or hashtag (e.g., #zeeter)
- **Sort**: Switch between "Newest" and "Trending" posts

## Tips
- Use hashtags (e.g., #zeeter) to categorize your posts
- Your bio can include any information you want to share
- You can change your avatar by entering a URL to an image
- Character count shows in real-time as you type
- Posts from users you follow will appear in future versions with a personalized feed
