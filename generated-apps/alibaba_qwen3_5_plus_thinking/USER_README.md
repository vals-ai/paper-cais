# Zeeter - User Guide

## Getting Started

### Quick Start
1. Open the application in your browser at the provided URL
2. Click "Sign Up" to create a new account
3. Enter your email and password (no email verification required)
4. You'll be automatically signed in and taken to your home feed

## Navigation Guide

### Main Pages

#### Home Feed (`/`)
- **View posts**: See the latest posts from the community
- **Create a post**: Use the text box at the top (280 character limit)
- **Sort feed**: Toggle between "Newest" and "Trending"
- **Search**: Find posts by keyword or username
- **Interact**: Like or comment on any post

#### Profile Page (`/profile/[userId]`)
- **View profile**: See a user's bio, join date, and all posts
- **Edit profile** (your profile only): Click "Edit Profile" to update:
  - Display name
  - Bio
  - Avatar URL
- **Follow/Unfollow**: Click the follow button on other users' profiles

#### Notifications (`/notifications`)
- View alerts for:
  - Likes on your posts
  - Comments on your posts
  - New followers
- Notifications are automatically marked as read when viewed

## User Roles

### Visitor (Not Signed In)
- Browse the public feed
- View user profiles
- Search for content
- Cannot like, comment, follow, or post

### Member (Signed In)
- All visitor capabilities, plus:
- Create and publish posts
- Like and comment on posts
- Follow other users
- Receive notifications
- Customize profile

## Tips
- Posts are limited to 280 characters
- You can edit or delete your posts at any time
- The character counter shows your progress as you type
- Notifications help you stay updated on interactions with your content
