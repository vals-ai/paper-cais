## Navigation Guide

### Key URLs
| URL | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| `/` | Home feed — browse posts, create new posts (when logged in) |
| `/login` | Sign in with email and password |
| `/signup` | Create a new account |
| `/explore` | Discover users and search posts |
| `/profile/:username` | View user profile and their posts |
| `/notifications` | View your notifications (likes, comments, follows) |

### Key Actions to Test

1. **Sign Up / Sign In**: Click "Sign Up" or "Log In" in the top-right navbar. Use the test credentials above or create a new account.

2. **Create a Post**: On the Home page, type in the "What's happening?" box (max 280 chars) and click "Post". Use #hashtags for discovery.

3. **Like a Post**: Click the heart icon on any post. The count updates instantly.

4. **Comment on a Post**: Click the comment (speech bubble) icon to expand comments. Type a comment and click send.

5. **Edit/Delete Posts**: On your own posts, click the "..." menu to edit or delete.

6. **Follow Users**: Go to Explore, find a user, and click "Follow". Their posts will appear in your feed.

7. **View Profile**: Click on any username or avatar to see their profile, bio, and posts. Edit your own profile by clicking the pencil icon.

8. **Upload Avatar**: On your profile page, hover over your avatar and click to upload a new image.

9. **Check Notifications**: The bell icon in the navbar shows unread count. Click to see who liked, commented, or followed you.

10. **Search**: Use the search bar on Home or Explore to find posts by keyword or #hashtag.
