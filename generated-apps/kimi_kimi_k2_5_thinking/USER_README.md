# Zeeter - User Guide

## Welcome to Zeeter!

Zeeter is a social network where you can share short posts, connect with others, and discover interesting content.

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Navigate to the application and click "Sign Up"
2. Enter your username, email, and password
3. Click "Create Account"
4. You'll be automatically logged in and taken to your home feed

### Default Test Account

For testing purposes, you can create a new account with any email and password. Email verification is not required.

## Navigation Guide

### Home Feed (`/`)
- View posts from users you follow and popular content
- Sort by "Newest" or "Trending"
- Create new posts (up to 280 characters)
- Like and comment on posts

### Search (`/search`)
- Search for posts by keyword or hashtag
- Search for users by username or display name
- Results show both posts and people tabs

### Profile (`/profile/:username`)
- View any user's profile
- See their posts, followers, and following count
- Follow/unfollow users
- Edit your own profile (display name, bio)

### Notifications (`/notifications`)
- View alerts for:
  - Likes on your posts
  - Comments on your posts
  - New followers
- Unread notifications are highlighted

### Post Detail (`/post/:postId`)
- View a single post with full conversation
- Add comments
- See who liked the post

## Key Workflows

### Creating a Post
1. From the home feed, type in the text box at the top
2. Keep it under 280 characters
3. Click the "Post" button

### Following Users
1. Visit any user's profile
2. Click the "Follow" button
3. Their posts will appear in your home feed

### Editing Your Profile
1. Go to your profile page
2. Click "Edit Profile"
3. Update your display name and bio
4. Click "Save"

### Managing Posts
- **Edit**: Click the three dots menu on your post → Edit
- **Delete**: Click the three dots menu on your post → Delete

## Tips

- Use hashtags in your posts to make them discoverable
- Check the Notifications page to stay updated on engagement
- Browse Trending to see popular content
- Your profile shows your activity and follower count
